# ULTRA GURU MD

A multi-device WhatsApp bot built by GURUTECH. Automates WhatsApp tasks including AI interactions, media downloading, group management, and more.

## Stack

- Runtime: Node.js 20
- WhatsApp: @whiskeysockets/baileys
- Database: PostgreSQL (Sequelize) with SQLite fallback
- Web: Express (pairing page on port 5000)
- Media: ffmpeg, sharp, jimp
